Inzaar Mobile App - Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 16, 2026
Last Updated: March 16, 2026
This Privacy Policy explains how the Inzaar mobile application ("Inzaar", "App", "we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our application.

Inzaar provides Islamic educational and informational content. Users can access the App without creating an account or logging in.

By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require registration or login to use the App. However, certain information may be collected automatically by the App and third-party services used within the App.

Information collected automatically may include:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system and version
  • App usage information
  • IP address
  • Advertising identifiers, such as Android Advertising ID
  • Crash logs and diagnostics information
  • Subscription status information needed to determine premium access

Information we do not directly collect:

  • Name, username, or password for account login, because no login system is provided
  • Payment card or banking details, because payments are processed through Google Play

2. How We Use Information

Information handled through the App may be used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App
  • To display ads to free users
  • To verify premium subscription status and remove ads for subscribed users
  • To improve performance, stability, and user experience
  • To diagnose crashes and fix technical issues
  • To prevent abuse, fraud, and security problems
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Advertising

The App may display advertisements to users who are not subscribed to the premium ad-free plan.

We may use third-party advertising services, such as Google AdMob, to serve ads. These services may collect or receive information from your device, including advertising identifiers, IP address, device information, and ad interaction information for ad delivery, ad measurement, and related purposes in accordance with their own policies.

Users with an active premium subscription will not be shown ads while their subscription remains active and properly recognized by the App.

4. In-App Purchases and Subscription

The App offers an optional monthly subscription that removes all ads from the App.

  • All payments are processed securely by Google Play Billing
  • We do not directly collect or store your debit card, credit card, or bank details
  • We may receive limited purchase status information required to confirm whether your premium subscription is active
  • Subscription management, cancellation, renewals, and refunds are handled according to Google Play policies

5. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party tools and services to support functionality, advertising, billing, diagnostics, analytics, and security.

Examples may include:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Play Billing
  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics

These third-party services may process information under their own privacy policies and terms.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited information with third-party service providers only when necessary to:

  • Serve advertisements
  • Process subscriptions and verify purchases
  • Maintain, improve, and secure the App
  • Comply with applicable law or legal process

7. Data Retention

Information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, including maintaining app features, resolving disputes, complying with legal obligations, and enforcing agreements.

8. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information related to the App. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Choices

  • You may stop using the App at any time
  • You may manage or cancel your subscription through your Google Play account
  • You may reset or limit advertising identifiers through your Android device settings
  • You may control certain device permissions and privacy settings through your device settings

10. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

If you believe that a child under 13 has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so that we can review and take appropriate action.

11. External Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites, including the Inzaar website. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or policies of third-party websites or services.

12. International Data Processing

Depending on the services used in the App, information may be processed on servers located outside your country. By using the App, you understand that information may be transferred to and processed in countries that may have different data protection laws from your own.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are made, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Your continued use of the App after any update becomes effective means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.

14. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us:

Inzaar
Website: https://www.inzaar.org/
Email: support@inzaar.org
